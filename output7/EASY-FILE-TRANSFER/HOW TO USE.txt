------HOW TO INSTALL -----
just double click on the EASY-FILE-TRANSFER.exe to install.



--------HOW TO USE IT--------
1. After installing the exe , just open EASY-FILE-TRANSFER folder ,where you have installed and double click on uploadpy.exe
2. Then a black console will open and a IP will be provided below.
3. Just use this IP on browser to render the application.

-------NOTICE------

1. IF you want to share your files then just copy your files to "EASY-FILE-TRANSFER/static/myfiles" directory

2. If you want to upload your files through our upload section, then after uploading the files gets stored in "EASY-FILE-TRANSFER/static/myfiles"
